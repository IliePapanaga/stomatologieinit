Questions and Answers

Question: I observe two assignments at the same day but in different locations. I have applied for one assignment and would like to apply for another one. However, I cannot. What is a reason?

Answer: A candidate is able to apply only for one posting for one day. One day – one available posting.

Question: I cannot apply for another posting. There is a message, that a candidate cannot apply for posting 10 minutes. Is it right?

Answer: Yes, that’s correct behavior. A candidate can apply for another posting only after 10 minutes late after the previous application. That’s is done for the dental offices that can book a candidate within these 10 minutes.

Question: I live in Dublin, CA and cannot see temporary assignments in San Jose, CA, but I would like to watch them. What should I do?

Answer: Go to Settings/General section and check option: “Radius willing to travel in miles from home location”. Please select the required distance.

Question: I do not observe any new assignments. What is a reason for it?

Answer: Please check status of your certificates. If any required certificate is rejected or expired, a professional cannot see any appropriate assignments.

Question: My assignment starts at 09:00 AM. I have arrived earlier, about 07:00 AM and I would like to make “Check In” order to not forget about it. However, “Check In” button is disabled.

Answer: “Check In” button is available for professional, one hour to start of assignment.

Question: I observe a permanent assignment with a status “You have an offer”, but I have not applied for this assignment earlier. What does it mean?

Answer: It means that a Candidate worked previously for some dental office and the dental office made a direct booking of the candidate.
