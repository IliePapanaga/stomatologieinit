6.1 Post Review

A professional is able to post review about a dental practice.

Note: Rating and Review provided by professionals are available only for MayDayDental Staffing staff.
  1. Navigate to Job History at the left navigation panel.
  2. Select an Assignment.

  3. Click on POST REVIEW button.
  4. Observe POST REVIEW dialog.
  5. Rate the dental practice and provide Comments if you have.
  6. Click OK button.
    Note: A doctor can also provide feedback about professionals in next categories:
    • Professionalism
    • Communication
    • Work Quality
    • Punctuality
    • Appearance
    Note: Total rating of a candidate is available for all doctors and helps to make a decision about hiring an appropriate candidate.
    Tip: If you want to work in the dental office permanently, you may check I would work here permanently box.
    Note: If a professional selected I would work here permanently , the notifications about it are sent to:
    • May Day Dental Staffing administrators;
    • Dental Practice where the assignemnt took place.
