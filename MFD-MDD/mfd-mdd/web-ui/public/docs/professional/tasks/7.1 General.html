7.1 General

Before starting search for a new assignment, a candidate should provide required information.

  1. Navigate to General at the Settings section at the left navigation panel.
  2. Observe General section.
  3. Upload your picture.
    Note: Supported types are JPEG, PNG. Size of uploaded picture is limited to 500 KB.
    Note: Please be advise that your image is available for dental practice.
  4. Select Preferences & Availability options.
    Note: Provided Desired Rate Per Hour ($) is available for dental practices.
    Note: Option Willing to relocate is not mandatory, however it is important to show a willingness of candidate to relocate and dental practices will see it.
    Note: Please note that selection next values impacts to the results of assignment search:
    • Radius willing to travel in miles from home location;
    • Willing to relocate allows a user to view all available permanent assignments in the application;
    • Availability (by days of a week).
