4.7 SOS

SOS is a case, when a professional has not arrived to a dental practice and a doctor requires urgently a replacement.

A doctor is able to escalate SOS action to MaydayDental Staffing office.

  1. Navigate to Current Attendance view.
  2. Select a required record.
  3. Click SOS button.
  4. Observe SOS dialog.
  5. Click OK button.
    Note: Mayday Dental Staffing Office receives an email notification about SOS action. For MaydayDental Staffing it means, that a dental office requires a professional urgently to replace the candidate. Mayday Dental Staffing Office starts to look for a potential candidate.
