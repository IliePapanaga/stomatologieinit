4.5 Alerts

.

In some cases, a doctor would like to alert a candidate.

A Doctor is able to send an alert notification to a candidate.

A Candidate receives such notification by email or sms, and if the candidate has a chance to reply, the candidate may answer with some short templates supported by applications.

Note: Please be aware, that a candidate may is in a drive to your location when he/she receives such alert and does not have an opportunity to reply you in a timely manner.
  1. Navigate to Current Attendance view.
  2. Select a required record.
  3. Click ALERT button.
  4. Observe ALERT dialog.
  5. Click OK button.
    Note: As a result, a notification (sms/email) is sent to the Professional with a context like: "Doctor is worried a little bit about your time arrival".
    Note: It is possible to initiate an alert action only one before a job starts and till one hour after job is started. For example, is Job starts at 09:00 am, a doctor is able to invoke Alert action from 08:00 am till 10:00 am. Otherwise validation message appears.
