3.2.5 Job Interview Statuses

There are next statuses after scheduling an interview.

Table 1.
Status Description
[No Status] A doctor has not scheduled an interview yet.
Invitation sent A doctor has scheduled an interview. A candidate has not replied yet. Notifications are sent to the candidate and Mayday Dental Staffing office.
Invitation accepted A candidate has accepted the suggested date and time for an interview. Notifications are sent to the Doctor and Mayday Dental Staffing office.
Invitation rejected A candidate has rejected the suggested options for the interview. However, the candidate is still applied for this position. Notifications are sent to the Doctor and Mayday Dental Staffing office. A doctor is able to schedule another interview date and time.
Cancelled A doctor has cancelled an interview.
