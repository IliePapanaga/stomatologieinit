Managing P3C activities

You can view, restart and delete the operations, e.g. provisioning, related to spaces.

Remember: P3C logs the history of 3 activity types, i.e. spaces, services, and environments with the services.
  1. Click Infrastructure Operations.
  2. Click the Activity view, select the check-box next to an activity you need, and click View Details.
    Tip: The type of an operation, i.e. an activity, is listed in the Operation column; if the operation is still underway, "processing" is displayed in the Status column. To update the status of an operation(s), click the Refresh icon at the bottom of the screen.
    The progress of an operation, for example, provisioning progress, is displayed at the top of the dialog box; to find out more about a specific step's status, or look up a failure description,click View Details.
Note: After an operation, e.g. provisioning a space or abandoning that space-provisioning operation, has been completed, the record about that operation is transferred from the Activities tab to the Activity History tab.
