4.2 Temporary Job Attendance Statuses

.

There are next available statuses for Job Attendance.
Table 1.
Status Description
Need check in A professional has not arrived yet and current time is less than time of beginning of a job.
Checked in There are two possible options:
  • A candidate arrived and a doctor has made Check in. Notifications are sent to Professional and MaydayDental Staffing office
  • If a Doctor has not made any other actions (Candidate is late, No Show or SOS), than a application changes a status to “Check In” automatically in one hour after posting starts . Notifications are sent to Doctor, Professional and MaydayDental Staffing office.
No Show A candidate has not arrived. The notification is sent to MaydayDental Staffing office and the Professional. After two “No Show”, a candidate is not able to see any new postings, however a candidate is able to see the assigned postings.
SOS A candidate has not arrived and a doctor urgently needs assistance to have another professional. Notification is sent to MaydayDental Staffing office with the highest importance. Notification is sent to Professional about this status.
Rejected A professional has arrived. However, a doctor has made rejection to this candidate due to some reasons.

Note:
Remember: If a Job Attendance is in Checked in Status, your Dental office is charged by the MaydayDental Staffing office automatically. See details about it 6.2 Payments for the Temporary Positions and 6.3 Payments for Permanent Positions.
