7.1.2 Change Email

A user is able to change his email address.

  1. Click Settings Tab at the Navigation Panel.
  2. Click General at Settings at the Navigation Panel.
  3. Click CHANGE button.
  4. Observe that CHANGE EMAIL dialog appears.
  5. Populate the required fields.
  6. Click OK button.
    Note: The notification about username modification is sent to previous email address.
